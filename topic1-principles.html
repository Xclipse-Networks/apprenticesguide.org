Topic 01 // Machine Principles

Energy
Conversion

Motors and generators play a vital role in the Electrotechnology industry. Motors convert electrical energy to mechanical energy and are used to provide the driving power for many electrical machines.

Generators convert mechanical energy to electrical energy which powers our modern society. This topic establishes the foundational physics required for your UEEEL0019 certification.

1.1 Introduction

Direct current (d.c.) motors can be used in a wide range of applications, and are commonly seen in the world of pumps, winches and hoists, HVAC (heating, ventilation and air conditioning), transport and gate or door automation.

Motor Role

Drive systems for mechanical displacement and rotation.

Generator Role

Primary source of society-scale electrical energy conversion.

"The design and construction of d.c. motors and generators are essentially the same. Their difference lies in the flow of energy."

Growth Sectors

  • Electric Mowers

Brushless Evolution

Brushless motors have many added benefits. They can be controlled at any speed, easily reversed, have less noise, higher power to weight ratio, higher efficiency and less maintenance.
